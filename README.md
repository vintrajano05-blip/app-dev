 My Favorite Series 🎬

 The Boys

![The Boys](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/5/5b/The_Boys_Season_3.jpg)

 Overview
*The Boys* is a superhero series, but not the typical kind. Instead of heroes always being good, it shows what could happen if people with superpowers abused their fame and influence. The story follows a group called “The Boys” who try to expose and stop these corrupt superheroes.

 Why I Like It ❤️
I like this series because it feels more realistic compared to other superhero shows. It shows that power can change people, and not always in a good way. The story is intense, sometimes shocking, but also really interesting. It keeps me curious about what will happen next.

 Main Characters 👥
- Billy Butcher  
- Hughie Campbell  
- Homelander  
- Annie January (Starlight)  
- Queen Maeve
